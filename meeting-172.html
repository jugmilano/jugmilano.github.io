Abstract dell'intervento:
As global energy demand surges, the transition to Renewable Energy Communities (RECs) and flexible markets is no longer optional, it’s a technical necessity. Join us as we take a deep dive into SPARklE, a cutting-edge platform designed to orchestrate the future of energy. We’ll break down the architecture behind the system, from ingesting high-frequency IoT data to managing multi-tenant security through PostgreSQL Row-Level Security. You’ll learn how we balanced the complexities of energy forecasting with a robust DevOps pipeline using Pulumi, FluxCD, and Kubernetes. Whether you're interested in Green-Tech or high-scale data engineering, this talk offers a roadmap for building resilient systems in an ever-evolving energy landscape.

A cura di Michele Giacobazzi:
Michele Giacobazzi
Michele is CTO and Founder in WellD Italia, a software company born in 2008, focused on delivering cutting-edge solutions across various industries. With a strong foundation in Java, Michele brings expertise in development practices and methodologies to the company’s software projects and teams.
He has a strong experience on the energy domain, starting from distribution and recharging infrastructure to renewable energy monitoring and optimization.
